IMPACT OF TECHNOLOGY

What is technology really? The term technology is derived from the ancient Greek words “techne” and “logos”, which translates to “art, craft” and “word, speech” respectively. The term was used for the first time in the 17th century, and it was denoted to mean “a discussion of the applied arts”. Due to rapid advancement of technology it is more than it used to be.


Technology is everywhere around us. We use technology everyday to make our work easier and faster. In my life as well, I use a lot of technologies and for different purpose. As a student I use different technologies like smart phones, computers, televisions, gaming consoles, bikes and many more

As a student, technology like computers,smart phones, watches has been a crucial part of my life. Without them I cannot move on with my day. As a student my primary focus is education and without these technologies, I cannot get my education.Computers help me do my homeworks,research on different topics for my school work, check my emails and many more. Since the pandemic, it has been more important than ever. All the classes have been taught digitally so without computer I would have a tough time doing my school work. Similary smart phones help me achieve the same goals as well.It also helps me do my research, connect and interact with my friends and teachers. I also wear watch everyday to make sure I am always on time wherever I go.


Furthermore, I use technologies like televisions, gaming consoles for my entertain- ment purposes. I enjoy watching tv shows and movies in television and I also have fun playing games with my friends which is all technology. Studying and doing school work can make us tired and exhaust so using these technologies I can get a break from my studies and can enjoy some time. Using of these technologies can make a person be addicted to them and wasting a lot of time.So i always make sure to use these technologies responsibly.I also use technologies like refrigerator to store my foods and washing machine to wash my clothes. For travelling purposes I use my bike to go places that I have to be in which saves a lot of my time and it is the easiest transportation medium in my opinion.

To conclude, I would say that technologies have been an important part of my life.The amount of time it saves and how it makes my work easier; it has a huge impact in my life. The benefits I get from technologies as a student and as a human is tremendous.
